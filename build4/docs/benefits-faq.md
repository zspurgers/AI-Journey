# Benefits FAQ

**Company:** Meridian Software
**Last updated:** January 2026

## When does health insurance start?

Health, dental, and vision coverage begin on the **first day of the month after your start date**. If you start on the 1st, coverage begins that same day.

## What health plans are offered?

- **PPO Plan** — higher premium, lower deductible, larger network
- **HDHP Plan** — lower premium, higher deductible, comes with an HSA

Meridian contributes $1,200 per year to your HSA if you choose the HDHP plan.

## 401(k)

Meridian matches **100% of the first 4%** you contribute. You are eligible to enroll after 60 days of employment. Matching contributions vest immediately.

## Parental leave

Birth parents receive **12 weeks** of paid parental leave. Non-birth parents receive **6 weeks**. Leave must be taken within the first year of the child's birth or adoption.

## Remote work

Meridian is hybrid. Most roles are expected in-office **2 days per week**. Fully remote arrangements require VP approval.
