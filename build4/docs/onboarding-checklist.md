# New Hire Onboarding Checklist

**Company:** Meridian Software
**Last updated:** January 2026

## Before your first day

- Complete your I-9 and W-4 in Workday
- Set up direct deposit in Workday
- Watch for your laptop shipment (arrives 2–3 days before start)

## Week 1

- **Day 1:** Meet your manager, get your building badge, set up your laptop and MFA
- **Day 2:** IT orientation — email, Slack, VPN, and the internal wiki
- **Day 3:** Benefits enrollment session (you have 30 days from your start date to enroll)
- **Day 4:** Meet your onboarding buddy, review your 30-day goals
- **Day 5:** Security and compliance training (required, ~2 hours)

## First 30 days

- Complete all assigned training in the Learning Portal
- Schedule 1:1s with your immediate team
- Enroll in benefits before your 30-day deadline
- Enroll in the 401(k) once you hit 60 days of employment

## Who to ask

- **Payroll and PTO questions:** the People Team (people@meridian.example)
- **Laptop and access issues:** IT Help Desk (Slack: #it-help)
- **Anything else:** your onboarding buddy or manager
