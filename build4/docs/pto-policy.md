# Paid Time Off (PTO) Policy

**Company:** Meridian Software
**Last updated:** January 2026

## How much PTO do I get?

- **New hires (0–2 years):** 15 days of PTO per year
- **3–5 years:** 20 days per year
- **6+ years:** 25 days per year

PTO accrues monthly. A new hire earns 1.25 days per month.

## Rollover

You can roll over a maximum of 5 unused PTO days into the next calendar year. Any unused days beyond 5 are forfeited on December 31.

## Requesting time off

Submit PTO requests in Workday at least **two weeks in advance** for anything longer than two days. Your manager approves or declines within 3 business days.

## Sick leave

Sick leave is separate from PTO. All employees get **7 paid sick days** per year. Sick days do not roll over.

## Holidays

Meridian observes 11 paid company holidays per year. The holiday schedule is posted in Workday each January.
